  1. Select your Goal and Timeline. The shorter timeline has a higher score associated with the variables (i.e. the impact of your choices is greater).

  2. Depending on your chosen goal, a set of scenarios will be presented in the form of multi-choice questions along the chosen timeline. The choices you make affect the value of the variables.

  3. There are 5 progress bar variables, namely:

    • Your Health
    • Babyâ€™s Health
    • Savings
    • *Skills
    • Happiness

  4. You must ensure that the variable values do not fall below a threshold value at which a threshold question/scenario will be presented for you to recover. A maximum of 2 threshold values per variable are permitted, failing which you will be unsuccessful with your goal.

  5. At the end of the timeline, your overall score will be calculated as a weighted average of all the dependent variables which will be scaled to a maximum value of 100 points.

  6. There are 3 possible outcomes to this game:

    • Successful: overall score > 74
    • Almost There: overall score > 50
    • Try Again: overall score < 51
